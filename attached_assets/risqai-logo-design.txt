RisqAi Logo Design Guidelines
----------------------------
Brand Name: RisqAi (with period)
Pronunciation: "risk-eye" (like "risk AI")

Logo Elements:
1. Word Mark: "RisqAi."
   - "Risq" in white (#FFFFFF)
   - "Ai" in cyan (#00FFFF)
   - Period in purple (#BF00FF)
   - Font: Space Grotesk Bold

2. Color Treatments:
   - Standard: As described above
   - Gradient: Purple-to-cyan gradient on full wordmark
   - Monochrome: All white for dark backgrounds
   - Inverted: Dark text for light backgrounds

3. Logo Spacing:
   - Minimum clear space: Equal to the height of the "i" in "Ai"
   - Do not crowd the logo with other visual elements
   - Keep consistent padding in all applications

4. Logo Sizes:
   - Minimum size: 80px width for digital, 1 inch for print
   - Header size: 120-180px width
   - Favicon/app icon: Use the period symbol only

5. Usage Guidelines:
   - Always maintain the original proportions
   - Do not distort or stretch the logo
   - Do not change the relative sizes of "Risq" and "Ai"
   - Do not change the brand colors outside approved palette
   - Do not add effects like drop shadows (except the approved glow)

6. Digital Applications:
   - Website header: Gradient version with subtle glow effect
   - Favicon: Purple dot
   - Social media: Standard version with clear space
   - Email signatures: Standard version, 120px width

7. Logo Animation (when applicable):
   - Subtle cyan glow pulse on the "Ai" portion
   - Duration: 2-3 seconds per pulse cycle
   - Optional gradient flow animation for special applications
